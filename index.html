Welcome To The Weller House In Fort Bragg, CA

Fort Bragg, CA

The Weller House is the oldest house, in the oldest neighborhood in the historic and storied coastal town of Fort Bragg, CA. Sleep, think, and unwind in the re-imagined 1886 Victorian Italianate home and water tower. Dance, meditate, and retreat to the 900 square foot virgin redwood ballroom. Explore, comb, and experience the magic of the place where the redwoods, meet the rivers, meet the wild Northern California sea.

Rooms & Amenities

The Weller House features 7 rentable bedrooms with private baths in the main house, plus 4 rentable studios in the water tower. Each room is individually designed and thoughtfully appointed, with an eclectic, modern aesthetic, to complement the historical details of the home.

Rooms feature high-end mattresses, luxurious linens, original artwork, antique furniture, sustainably sourced and all natural toiletries, and some have claw-foot or jacuzzi bath tubs. Breakfast is sourced from nearby farms and bakeries and feature homemade jam and perfectly roasted artisan coffee and tea.

Events, Retreats & Workshops

Our ballroom provides the perfect gathering place for weddings, family reunions, company parties, or other special events. With special buy-out packages we will help arrange all of the details to make your day a one of a kind experience. The Weller House is a refuge from the fast pace and noise of the modern world, yet still fully connected with high speed internet and modern amenities. It is the perfect place for art, writing, corporate, or yoga retreats.
